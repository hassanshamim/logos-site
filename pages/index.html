Our Mission

Logos Language Center is a nonprofit adult education organization dedicated to providing language learners with affordable, holistic language instruction through an innovative cultural lens. Our collaborative approach enables learners to define and express themselves with total communicative competency. Our multi-prism curriculum addresses academic preparedness, professional growth, cultural enrichment, and social engagement.

Where?

Despite originating from all over the US, the teachers at Logos have all decided to call Portland, Oregon home. From the delicious food carts to the fantastic parks and hiking trails, it wasn't hard for Portland to win us over. When deciding to start a non-profit ESL school, we knew nowhere would welcome us better than the passionate folks that populate Portland.

Our Summer Program

This summer we will be offering 4 weeks of immersive English activities for international students. The camp will be a fully immersive atmosphere, teaching English and culture through sensory experiences. Our four 1-week camps will each have a different theme. This summer our themes will be "The Culture of Food", "Urban Parks, Nature, and Conservation", "Storytelling", and "American Culture in Film".
